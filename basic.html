I love Cars

I really like cars

Recently I have REALLY liked cars and gotten into them heavily.
I have started working on them with my dad recently and I have been having fun with tinkering with my car ever since.

Why I like working on them

  • It is fun figuring out how to take off and put on different parts
  • I get to go deeper into my own car and can work on and know more about my own car
  • I like working with my hands and my car is a great way for me to work with both my mind and hands

How I changed my headlights

  1. I had to remove my front bumper
  2. Un-wire the wires from earlier headlights and remove the lights themselves
  3. Hook the new wires in then place bumper back on
  4. Finally fit back on the new headlights

My conclusion

In conclusion I really love working on cars and I am grateful to my dad for introducing me into cars.
